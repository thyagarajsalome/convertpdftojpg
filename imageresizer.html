Benefits of Image resizer

  • ✅ Fast and efficient image compression without significant quality loss.
  • ✅ Save storage space on your devices and websites.
  • ✅ Improve website loading speed by reducing image file sizes.
  • ✅ Easy drag-and-drop interface with instant compression results.
  • ✅ Download all compressed images in one click as a ZIP file.
  • ✅ No installation needed — completely online and secure.

How to Use

  1. Click the drop area or drag and drop your JPG images into the highlighted box.
  2. Select the desired reduction scale (e.g., 75%, 50%, or 25%) from the dropdown menu.
  3. Click the Compress button to start resizing your images.
  4. Review the compression results for each image.
  5. Click the Download button to save all compressed images as a ZIP file.
